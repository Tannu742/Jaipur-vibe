15 January - 19 January 2026

The world's grandest celebration of books and idea

Join us for a spectacular journey into books, ideas, and creativity — right here, from 15-19 January 2026!

The 2025 edition of the Jaipur Literature Festival will continue its tradition of fostering literary thought and engaging with the most pertinent global themes. Bringing together leading voices from literature, politics, science, and the arts, the Festival will be a forum for diverse perspectives and meaningful exchange. The festival will be the premier space for intellectual engagement, where ideas are discussed and stories shared.

Become a Friend of the Festival and access the finest of the facilities that the iconic Jaipur Literature Festival has to offer!

Registration

The 2025 edition of the Jaipur Literature Festival will continue to foster literary dialogue, bringing together leading voices from literature, politics, science, and the arts. Register now for the 18th edition at Hotel Clarks Amer, Jaipur, with insightful sessions, performances, book signings, and the simultaneously held Jaipur BookMark and Jaipur Music Stage.

Join Us Online!

Jaipur BookMark (JBM) brings together stakeholders of the book trade from across the world – from publishers to literary agents, writers, translators, translation agencies and booksellers. It gives them an opportunity not just to talk business (through panel discussions and roundtables), but also provides the right and fruitful atmosphere to inspire one-on-one meetings and networking.

Our Partners

Jaipur Music Stage

Jaipur Music Stage, held for three evenings at the close of the day’s happenings at Jaipur Literature Festival, is a celebration of music with leading international and Indian musicians at the Hotels Clarks Amer grounds. A multi-genre music festival spread over 3 days, the Jaipur Music Stage has a diverse bouquet of folk, fusion, rock and world music.
